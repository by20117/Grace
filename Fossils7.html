Fossils: 7th Grade

Overview:




One of my 7th grade subjects was Fossils. Fossils is the study of the remains of once alive organisms. My partner was an 8th grader named Agastya. We were given a Science Olympiad Official Fossil List. The fossil list changes a bit each year to provide new information.

Rules:

  • Each team gets to have a 2-inch ring binder that contains all of their research. The supervisors are very serious about this. They sometimes check each binder and if a binder is bigger than it is allowed to be, the team will be disqualified or forced to use only their memories. The binder's contents can be anything regarding the event.
  • During the test, there will be stations laid out on different tables. You can only visit each station once. Each station will be timed between 1-4 minutes. If you do not finish, you still have to move on. You are not allowed to go back to any past stations.
  • You may not take out any pages from your binder during the test. Everything you look at has to be firmly attached to the binder.
